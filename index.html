About Me

I'm Katie Gray, a physics graduate student working on the ALICE experiment at CERN. My research focuses on Quantum Chromodynamics and the thermal and collective behavior of matter at extremely high energy densities. I'm passionate about science communication, teaching, and community outreach.

Research

I work on heavy-ion physics within the ALICE Collaboration at the LHC. My recent focus includes studying the QCD phase diagram and applying statistical and machine learning methods to understand jet quenching and other collective phenomena. Previously, I worked on laser-based atom interferometry and quantum sensors.
